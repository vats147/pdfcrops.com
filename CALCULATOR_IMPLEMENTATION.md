### Meesho
- Commission: 15% (average)
- Payment Gateway: 2%
- GST on Commission: 18%

### Amazon
- Referral Fee: 5-15% (category-dependent)
- FBA Fee: ₹39-200+ (weight-based)
- Closing Fee: ₹25-50
- Payment Processing: 2%

- Commission: 4-18% (category-dependent)
- Shipping: ₹42-115 (zone & weight-based)
- Collection Fee: ₹20
- Fixed Fee: ₹20-40

- Final Value Fee: 8-15%

- Payment Processing: 3%
- International Fee: 1%

- Customs Duty: 7.5-60% (category-dependent)
- Shipping: ₹50-500/kg (method-dependent)
- GST: 18% on landed cost
- Clearance: ₹2,000-5,000
